Anatomy of a record

Each path is a directed drug→…→disease multigraph. Nodes are CURIE-identified and Biolink-typed; edges are Biolink predicates joined by node id. Newer records carry per-edge evidence.

graph:
  _id: DB00570_MESH_D013736_2
  drug: Vinblastine        drugbank: DB00570
  disease: Malignant tumor of testis   disease_mesh: MESH:D013736
nodes:
  - id: MESH:D014747     label: Drug       name: Vinblastine
  - id: UniProt:P07437   label: Protein    name: Tubulin beta chain
  - id: MESH:D013736     label: Disease    name: Malignant tumor of testis
links:
  - key: decreases activity of   source: MESH:D014747   target: UniProt:P07437
    evidence:
      snippet: "Vinblastine binds to tubulin dimers, inhibiting assembly of microtubules."
      publication: PMID:12042791
references:
  - https://go.drugbank.com/drugs/DB00570#mechanism-of-action
directed: true     multigraph: true
